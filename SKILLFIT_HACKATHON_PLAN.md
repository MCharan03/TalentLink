# Hackathon Submission Plan: AI SkillFit (Theme 5)

## 1. Project Identity
- **Name:** Aura SkillFit: The Sentient Workforce Layer
- **Theme:** Theme 5 - AI SkillFit: Video Assessment for Workforce Fitment
- **Tagline:** Bridging Karnataka’s Skill Gap through Dialect-Aware AI & Proof-of-Action Verification.

## 2. The "Out-of-the-Box" Innovation
*We are not building an interview app; we are building a trust-infrastructure for the Karnataka Government.*

- **Janani AI (Dialect-Native Persona):** Most AI feels "robotic" and intimidating. Janani uses a maternal, nurturing tone and automatically switches to the candidate's regional Kannada dialect to maximize response quality.
- **Computer Vision "Skill-Check":** For an electrician, the AI doesn't just ask "Can you wire a plug?". It asks them to "Show me how you hold a tester." The system uses real-time CV to assess hand stability and grip-confidence.
- **Haptic Integrity Detection:** Uses the mobile's accelerometer to detect hand-shivers or "shady" movements, feeding into a "Human Authenticity Score."
- **District Geospatial Intelligence:** A dashboard that doesn't just show lists, but a "Heatmap of Readiness," allowing the government to see exactly which taluk needs a new training center.

## 3. Core Challenge Solutions

| Challenge | Our Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Language Barriers** | Multi-modal transformer models trained on 12+ Kannada dialects + Hindi/English. |
| **High-Volume Intake** | Serverless "Auto-Scaling" architecture with asynchronous video processing. |
| **Authenticity/Fraud** | Neural Liveliness Shield (3D Head Pose + Eye Tracking) to stop deepfakes. |
| **Blue-Collar Fitment** | NLP-based skill extraction that maps informal talk to formal trade categories. |

## 4. Technical Architecture
- **Frontend:** React-Native / Mobile-Web with `Aura.css` for high-end aesthetic.
- **AI Core:** Gemini 1.5 Pro for deep transcript analysis; Mediapipe for real-time CV.
- **Speech:** Custom STT (Speech-to-Text) pipeline optimized for noisy, real-world rural environments.
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for structured data; Vector DB (Chroma) for similarity-based duplicate detection.

## 5. Judge's "Wow" Factors (Improvisable)
- **"The Ghost Interview":** Demonstrate how the AI handles a candidate who goes silent or loses connection, automatically resuming with a "Welcome back, don't worry!" prompt.
- **"Deep-Fake Defeat":** Show the system flagging a video when a photo is held up to the camera.
- **"Dialect Switch":** Demonstrate the AI switching from formal Kannada to a North Karnataka dialect mid-stream.

## 6. Workforce Classification Matrix
1. **Tier A (Alpha):** Job-Ready. Immediate placement in high-spec polytechnic roles.
2. **Tier B (Beta):** Skill-High, Language-Low. Needs communication training.
3. **Tier C (Gamma):** Potential. Needs 3-6 months vocational upskilling.
4. **Tier D (Delta):** Unverified/Duplicate. Flagged for manual audit.
